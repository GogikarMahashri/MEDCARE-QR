# **App Name**: MedCare QR

## Core Features:

- Patient Profile: Display patient information including photo, name, age, gender, blood group, health diseases, symptoms, contact number, email, doctor's name, hospital name, last consultation date, and next consultation date.
- QR Code Generation: Generate a QR code that encodes the patient's medical information for easy access by healthcare providers.
- Medication Schedule: Display a daily medication schedule with color-coded tablets (Morning-Sun, Afternoon-Half Sun, Night-Moon) and reminder notifications (bell, flash, vibration).
- Voice Assistant: A voice assistant that reads out medicine names, dosage, and timings in the patient's preferred language.
- Symptom Checker: Allow patients to describe symptoms; use AI as a tool to analyze and interpret their condition and provide possible causes to consider with their healthcare provider.
- Doctor's Dashboard: Enable doctors to log in and access patient medical history when QR code is scanned. They can update the details on the system as needed.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Gentle light blue (#ADD8E6) to evoke a sense of calm and trust, crucial for a healthcare app.
- Background color: Very light, desaturated blue (#F0F8FF) to maintain a clean and unobtrusive backdrop.
- Accent color: Soft lavender (#E6E6FA) as a secondary highlight to create subtle visual interest and emphasize important elements.
- Font: 'PT Sans', a humanist sans-serif offering readability and a modern yet approachable aesthetic.
- Use clear, universally recognizable icons for medication times, notification settings, and patient information. Ensure sufficient contrast.
- Slides designed to display Patient Profile, Medication Schedule, and Symptom Checker.
- Subtle animation on successful QR scan.
